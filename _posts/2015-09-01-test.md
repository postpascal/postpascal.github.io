Fourier Transform:

$$F(\omega)=\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}f(\tau)e^{-j\omega\tau}d\tau$$

$$F(\omega)=\mathscr{F}[f(t)]$$

Inverse Fourier Transform:

$$f(t)=\frac{1}{2\pi}\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}F(\omega)e^{j\omega t}d\omega$$

$$f(t)=\mathscr{F}^{-1}[F(\omega)]$$
